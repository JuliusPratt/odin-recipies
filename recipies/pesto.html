Pesto

Discription

Basic pesto made with basil, olive oil, pine nuts, and Parmesan cheese. This recipe differs from the classic preparation by the addition of parsley. You can add a little extra Parmesan to the mixture if you like.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups packed fresh basil leaves
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup pine nuts
  • 4 cloves garlic, or to taste
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice, or more to taste (optional)

Directions

  1. Combine basil, Parmesan cheese, olive oil, pine nuts, and garlic in the bowl of a food processor or blender.
  2. Blend to a smooth paste.
  3. Add lemon juice, if desired, and quickly pulse to combine.
